Shopping

You'll be needing a handful of items for any pet, and this is especially true for a Uromastyx. In this part of the care guide, you'll be finding a list of things you will need to buy. This of course won't include all that you need, additional research is strongly advised.

Lizard Tank

Your lizard will need a place to live before becoming comfortable enough to be brought out. For Uromastyx, you will need a 30 or 40 gallon tank.

More Info

Substrate

A substrate is the substance that your Uromastyx will be living on. Personally, I prefer Walnut Shell substrates, as they are 100% edible by lizards.

More Info

Decorations

Your Uromastyx will need somethings to interact wil, to climb on and hide under. Try to replicate the environment of a desert, some fake plants and driftwood.

More Info

UV and Heat Lamps

Lizards need heat to survive and heat to stay active, and a UV lamp so that they don't develop metabolic bone disease. A temperature gun is also required.

More Info

Bowl and Supplements

Your lizard needs a bowl to eat out of, cutting up a paper plate will do, but there are actual bowls you can get. Supplements are also needed.

More Info

Your Uromastyx

Uromastyx are tough to track down, they're easy to buy online but many of these sites tend to take bad care of their pets, we recommend contacting a local pet store.
