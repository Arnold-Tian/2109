Welcome!

To the ultimate care guide for your pet uromastyx.

This website will show you exactly how to treat your uromastyx in as little time as possible. If this is your first uromastyx, consider this a guide to help you fully understand the process of purchasing these scaled friends. It's also important that you do NOT treat this as a care guide for anything but a uromastyx.

What are Uromastyx?

Understanding your new scaley friend.

Uromastyx are desert dwelling lizards who have just recently entered the lizard pet scene. Other names of these include Uromastyx Geyri or Spiny Tailed Lizards. Uromastyx generally live in extremely warm temperatures and survive off of a plant based diet. This means that carrying for uromastyx is simpler than other lizards as unlike other lizards you do not have to feed bugs or rodents.
